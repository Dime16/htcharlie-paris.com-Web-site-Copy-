Choix du bracelet

The GR-great hike-is our first time-tested watch and nature that you can take everywhere without being afraid to spoil it. We wanted to offer you a watch with a design inspired by the past but with a sporting character resolutely modern. The GR responds to technical constraints adapted to any journey from a weekend hike between buddies up to a roadtrip of one month in South America.

  • INFORMATION

    • Made in France: Watch assembly in Besançon
    • Movement:Citizen Miyota caliber 1S13
    • Box: 316l Steel
    • Casing Diameter:Sapphire, ultra-resistant, anti-glare
    • Thickness of the case: 9mm
    • Warranty: 24 months
    • Sealing: 10ATM
    • Width of bracelet: 20mm

  • MANUFACTURING

    • This watch has been designed and manufactured in France in the purest tradition of French watchmaking know-how. By its history, France is the real cradle of European watchmaking. We therefore wish to nurture and develop this wonderful heritage by combining both the times of tradition and modernity.

MATTHEW WRINGER, AMBASSADOR OF THE GR

To present this watch, we have associated ourselves with the adventurer Matthew Wringer, who embodies to perfection the spirit of the GR. On this watch, whose case is 39mm in diameter, we have laid pretty and legible Arabic numerals as well as sword-shaped needles covered with Super-LumiNova ® which allow to read the time in the dark. A railway of seconds and minutes runs through the dial with small touches of Super-LumiNova ® over the hours.

NYLON BRACELET

To let the skin breathe, we have developed ultra resistant nylon bangles with a thickness of 2.5 mm which are perfectly adapted to the sporting practice while remaining sober and elegant.
